    The abstract pixelated blocks represent a novel form of system language in the digital era, utilized to simulate natural habitats in both the physical and virtual domains. These blocks illustrate the pervasive aggressive development and activities of human beings, thereby exposing the consequential erosion and disturbance of natural environments caused by societal and civilizational forces.
